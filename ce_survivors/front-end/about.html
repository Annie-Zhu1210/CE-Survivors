About SafeCity London

SafeCity London is a public safety initiative created by a team of London students concerned about personal safety in the city. Our platform transforms complex crime data into clear, actionable insights.

As students who regularly commute to areas like Stratford—known for street crime—we understand the importance of having accurate, accessible safety information at your fingertips.

What We Provide

At-a-Glance Safety Overview

See a color-coded map of London boroughs, with safety levels weighted for population density. Instantly identify safer areas (Green) and hotspots (Red).

Deep-Dive Analytics

Click on any borough to explore detailed crime trends. Filter by street, crime type, and time period with our easy-to-use interactive charts.

Personalized Safety Planning

Search crime rates based on your specific needs — whether you're walking alone at night, planning a trip, or considering a new neighborhood. Make informed decisions about your safety.
