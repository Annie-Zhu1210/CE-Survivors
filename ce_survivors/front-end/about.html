About CrimeMap London

CrimeMap London is a public safety initiative created by a team of London students concerned about personal safety in the city. Our platform transforms complex crime data into clear, actionable insights.

As students who regularly commute to areas like Stratford—known for street crime—we understand the importance of having accurate, accessible safety information at your fingertips.

What We Provide

At-a-Glance Safety Overview

See a color-coded map of London boroughs, with safety levels weighted for population density. Instantly identify safer areas (Green) and hotspots (Red).

Deep-Dive Analytics

Click on any borough to explore detailed crime trends. Filter by street, crime type, and time period with our easy-to-use interactive charts.

Personalized Safety Planning

Search crime rates based on your specific needs - whether you're walking alone at night, planning a trip, or considering a new neighborhood. Make informed decisions about your safety.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike existing public data visualizations with clunky interfaces and poor mobile display, we've prioritized:

  • Mobile-First Design: A seamless experience on your phone when you need it most.
  • Clarity Over Clutter: Clean aesthetics and legibility that existing solutions lack.
  • Context-Aware Data: We don't just show raw numbers; we adjust for population density to give a more accurate safety picture.

Transparent & Trustworthy Data

All our data is sourced directly from the official UK Police API, ensuring accuracy and reliability. We provide coordinates for approximate street locations of reported crimes, along with crime type and timeframe information.

Technical Architecture

This site visualizes London borough crime counts and allows drilling down by borough and location.

Built with HTML, CSS, vanilla JavaScript and a simple Node.js backend (no frameworks).
